BILASPUR

Bilaspur is a district of Himachal Pradesh, India . Its headquarters are in the town of Bilaspur . The district has an area of 1,167km² and population of 381,956. As of 2011 it is the third least populous district of Himachal Pradesh after Lahul and Spiti and Kinnaur.
