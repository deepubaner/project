MANDI

Mandi is a major town and a municipal corporation in Mandi district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is situted 145km north of state capital shimla in the north west Himalayas at an average alitutude of 880m and experinces pleasent summers and cold winters . Mandi is connected to Pathankot through national highwayb20 wich is almost 220km long and Manali to Chandigahr through National highway 21 which is 323km long . Mandi dictrict is 2nd largest economy in the state after kangra . This city is establish im 1527 by Ajber Sen as the seat of the Mandi state ,a pricely state till 1948. It is wellknown for its INTERNATIONAL MANDI SHIAVRATRI FAIR. Its is also the first heritage city of Himachal Pradesh
